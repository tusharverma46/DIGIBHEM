VARIETIES

Spinach

Organic Spinach is renowned for its tender, dark green leaves and earthy flavor.

Tomatoes

Organic tomatoes are free from harmful chemicals and toxins, plus they are also rich in antioxidants.

Potatoes

Organic potatoes are 80% water and 20% solids. They are one of the best sources of carbohydrates, fiber and minerals.

Broccoli

Organic broccoli are actually the flowers of a cabbage plant. It is sweet and tender enough to be eaten raw. Broccoli loves cheese, egg, almonds, garlic and cream.

Brinjals

Organic Brinjals are rich in vitamins and minerals such as vitamin C, vitamin K, potassium, folate, and manganese.

Carrot

Organic carrots are made up of 88% water, 7% sugar. While the remaining is protein, ash and almost negligible fat.
